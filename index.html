Modpack

Our modpack is fully optimized and already includes mods for optimization. All you need to do is download the ZIP archive and extract it into the mods folder in your Minecraft directory. The mods are for version 1.20.1 Forge. The pack also features a wide variety of weapons, armor, blocks, and technologies, including Create.
